Faiz ul Mawaid ul Burhaniyah (Poona Students)

  • 2009-2011

    Heading1

    "Farzando ne thaali nai kari ? Farzando ne to pehla thaali jawu jove" (not exact alfaaz) - These were the kalemaat nooraniyah which wrote the destiny of FMB Poona Students.

  • March 2011

    Heading2

    It was Poona Safar Mubarak of 1433 H, and Bawasaheb Moula Sayedna Mohammed Burhanuddin (RA), had graciously given Raza mubarak for a special qadambosi bethak for students. During this bethak the students did FMB niaz araz in Moulana (RA), with the niyat that we soon start receiving this azeem barkat. After the qadambosi of Burhanuddin Moula (RA), we proceeded to do the qadambosi of Sayedna Aaliqadr Moula (TUS). It was destined that we receive this barakaat of Burhanuddin Moula on the hands of His Mansoos. During qadambosi, Aaliqadr Moula asked a student if he recieved thaali, when the answer was not affirmative, Moula (TUS) gave the above irshaadaat.

  • December 2012

    Heading3

    Efforts were then made under the qiyadat of Shz Taha Bhaisaheb Najmuddin that the students themselves organize a Faiz, so that not only they receive the barakaat of thaali but also get involved in this azeem khidmat. For next three days Mufaddal Moula would say shifa on aata, out of which jaman was prepared and sent out to students in thaali. It was an ain Mojiza of both Moula that during the Safar Mubarak itself the thaali was organized and it started reaching students.

  • July 2014

    Heading4

    Come lets sip from this barakaat of both Moulas, lets eat every morsel and remember the shafaqat of Burhanuddin Moula (RA), and lets cherish each morsel and pray for a long life of his most precious gift - His Mansoos and our Bawasaheb Sayedna Abu Jafer us Saadiq Aaliqadr Mufaddal Saifuddin Moula (TUS).
